We Are HYPEthread

Where streetwear meets culture and self-expression

Our Origin

Founded in 2023 in the heart of the city, HYPEthread began as a passion project between two friends who saw streetwear as more than just clothing - but as wearable art that tells a story.

What started with hand-printed tees sold at local markets has grown into a movement that celebrates urban culture through bold designs and premium craftsmanship.
